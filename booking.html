Welcome to our little italian corner of Oslo.

Here at Resturant Italiano we strive to bring you the essence of italian cooking, as well as culture. We use fresh and locally sourced products, and of course, the best of italian wines.

We are a small resturant, with nine tables and a max capacity of 40 people. Therefore, we ask you to book a table, so that we may ensure our ability to accommodate you, and your party. You will find us at Josefines Gate 23, Oslo.

We very much look forward to seeing you.
